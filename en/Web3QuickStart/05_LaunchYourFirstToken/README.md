## Background

### What Is Pump.fun?

🚀 **Pump.fun** is a one-stop token creation and trading platform built on Solana, allowing anyone to launch their own token with minimal cost and barrier to entry. These tokens are typically called "meme coins" or "community coins" and can represent specific themes, events, or communities.

What makes Pump.fun unique:

| Feature | Description |
|:-------:|:-----------|
| Extremely Low Cost | Only 0.02 SOL needed to launch a token |
| User Friendly | No programming knowledge required — just a few clicks |
| Automatic Liquidity | Automatically creates a trading pair on Raydium |
| Instant Trading | Tradeable immediately after launch — no waiting |
| Community Interaction | Built-in commenting and social features |

### Why Launch a Meme on Solana?

⚡ The Solana blockchain offers these advantages:
- Ultra-high transaction speed (thousands of transactions per second)
- Extremely low transaction fees (typically under $0.01)
- Active ecosystem with a large user base
- Friendly environment for small token projects

## Prerequisites

### What You'll Need

Before starting, make sure you have the following:

1. **Solana Wallet**: We recommend [Phantom Wallet](https://phantom.app/)

   > 🔔 Phantom is one of the most popular Solana wallets, offering a user-friendly interface and robust security features.

2. **SOL**: At least 0.03 SOL (to cover the launch fee and initial purchase)
3. **Token Concept**: Including a name, symbol, description, and image

### Setting Up Phantom Wallet (If You Don't Have One)

1. Visit the [Phantom website](https://phantom.app/) and download the browser extension
2. Follow the prompts to create a new wallet
3. Make sure to securely back up your seed phrase
4. Transfer some SOL to your wallet (can be sent from an exchange)

> ⚠️ **Important**: The seed phrase is the only way to recover your wallet. Store it safely on paper and keep it in a secure location. Never share your seed phrase with anyone or store it on electronic devices.

## FAQ

### ❓ How much SOL is needed to launch a token?

A minimum of 0.03 SOL is required, including 0.02 SOL for the launch fee and 0.01 SOL for transaction fees. We recommend having at least 0.1 SOL so you can purchase some of your own tokens after launch.

### ❓ Can I modify the token information after launch?

Basic token information (such as name and symbol) cannot be modified after launch, but you can update social media links and participate in community discussions.

#### ❓ How can I increase my token's value?

- Create practical use cases and a roadmap
- Build an active community
- Conduct regular updates and marketing
- Seek partnerships and integration opportunities

#### ❓ How is token liquidity managed?

Pump.fun uses an automatic bonding curve to manage liquidity. As buying volume increases, the token price rises; as selling volume increases, the price falls. No manual liquidity provision is needed.

#### ❓ Why can't I find my token on a blockchain explorer after creating it?

After launch, your token needs a few minutes for Solana block explorers (such as Solscan or Solana Explorer) to update their indexes. You can use the Pump.fun interface to view your token information first while waiting for the block explorer to catch up.

#### ❓ Do I need to maintain my token? Are there recurring fees?

Tokens launched on Pump.fun don't require regular maintenance or ongoing fees. Once launched, your token will permanently exist on the Solana blockchain. The only costs are the initial launch fee and small network fees for transactions.
